For over 25 years

the Village Pub and Cafe has seasonally served the local community. Opening late May through the end of October, we provide visiters and locals of our community a quality dining experience, serving traditional American food and a variety of seafood. Located right next to the beautiful Pentwater Lake, we provide an outdoor dining experience that you do not often come by, with live musical entertainment Friday and Saturday evening. Our bar is also stocked with a respectable lineup of local Michigan, domestic, and imported beers; of which the local brews are always highly popular. We host a variety of events throughout the season, ensuring our customers have an enjoyable experience with us.
