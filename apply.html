Apply for a job!

The Village Pub and Cafe is a seasonal business, and so many workers do not return after working with us. We therefore are always looking for employees each new season, and take applications beginning late in the Spring and early in the summer. Send us your resume/application or stop in and fill one out if you would like one of the open positions. Experience is preferred but not required, as is TIPS certification or its equivalent.
